Research & Publications

Advancing the field through rigorous research in NLP, computer vision, and machine learning

Published 2024 NLP

Performance Analysis of LSTM and Bi-LSTM Models with Different Optimizers in Bangla Sentiment Analysis

SM Shaqib et al. | 2024

This research explores the effectiveness of LSTM and Bi-LSTM architectures for sentiment analysis in Bangla language text. We conducted comprehensive experiments with various optimization algorithms to identify the best-performing configurations. Our findings provide valuable insights for low-resource language processing and demonstrate significant improvements in sentiment classification accuracy through careful optimizer selection and hyperparameter tuning.

Published 2025 Computer Vision

Transfer Learning Techniques for Efficient Deep Neural Networks in Real-Time Strawberry Freshness Evaluation

SM Shaqib et al. | 2025

We present a novel approach to real-time agricultural quality assessment using transfer learning techniques. By leveraging pre-trained deep neural networks and fine-tuning them for strawberry freshness detection, we achieved high accuracy with significantly reduced training time and computational resources. This work has practical implications for automated quality control in food processing industries and supply chain management.

Published 2025 Explainable AI

A Transfer Learning Approach to Classify Insect Diversity Based on Explainable AI

SM Shaqib et al. | 2025

This paper introduces an explainable AI framework for insect classification that not only achieves high accuracy but also provides interpretable insights into model decision-making. Using transfer learning with attention mechanisms and visualization techniques, we enable researchers to understand which visual features drive classification decisions. This work contributes to biodiversity monitoring and ecological research by making AI models more transparent and trustworthy.
